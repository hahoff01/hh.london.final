Why Do We Need the Ocean? Why Should You Care?

  • The ocean produces over half of the world's oxygen

  • The ocean economy produces $282 billion in goods and services

  • It employs over 3 million people

  • 76% of all US trade involves some form of marine transportation

  • The annual estimated economic value of the ocean is $2.5 trillion

  • The ocean helps regulate climate

  • Many medical products come from ocean

  • It provides lots of food: More than just seafood, there are ingrediants from the ocean found in things like soymilk and peanut butter

  • A survey was done that showed that nearly 49 million adults over 18 participated in ocean and coastal recreation yearly. The money that they spent on this supported more than 3.1 part-time and full-time jobs

  • Without a healthy ocean we will not be able to survive
