What Can You Do?

Actions You Can Take as a Individual

  • Recycle more

  • Use less single use plastic

  • Use coral safe sunscreen

  • Buy sustainable seafood

  • Reduce your own carbon emissions and energy consumption

  • Use your vote to vote on ocean issues

  • When you're at the beach leave nothing behind

  • Support organizations that are working to protect the ocean

  • Pick up trash you see on the beach
